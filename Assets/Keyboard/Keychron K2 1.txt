The Keychron K2 is a compact 75% wireless mechanical keyboard that offers a versatile typing experience. It features Gateron switches (available in Red, Blue, or Brown) and supports both Bluetooth and USB-C connections. Its 4000mAh battery ensures long-lasting wireless use, and the hot-swappable switches allow for easy customization. The K2 is compatible with both Mac and Windows, and its RGB backlighting adds a touch of style to its minimalist design. It's a great choice for those who need a reliable, compact keyboard that works seamlessly across multiple devices.
