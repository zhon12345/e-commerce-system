The Ducky One 2 Mini is a 60% compact mechanical keyboard that’s especially popular among gamers and minimalists. It comes with Cherry MX switches (Red, Blue, Brown, Silent Red, or Silver), providing a variety of typing feels. Its detachable USB-C cable ensures easy portability, and the PBT double-shot keycaps offer long-lasting durability. The RGB lighting is fully customizable with Ducky’s software, allowing you to create unique lighting effects. Its small size makes it perfect for those who need more desk space while still wanting a high-quality typing experience.
