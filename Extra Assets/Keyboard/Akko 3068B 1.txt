The Akko 3068B is a 65% wireless mechanical keyboard that combines style and performance. It offers Akko’s proprietary switches (available in linear, tactile, or clicky variants) and supports Bluetooth, 2.4GHz, and USB-C connections. Its PBT keycaps come in various unique color schemes, adding to its visual appeal. The hot-swappable design allows for switch customization, and its long battery life ensures consistent wireless performance. It’s a stylish and functional choice for those seeking a wireless mechanical keyboard
